1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. Related technologies such as browser local storage work similarly. UK law (PECR, alongside UK GDPR) requires us to tell you what we use and to obtain consent for anything that is not strictly necessary.

2. What this site uses

This website is intentionally light. We set no advertising or tracking cookies. We use:

NameTypePurposeDuration
mvr-cookie-consent Local storage (strictly necessary) Remembers the choice you make on our cookie banner so we don't ask again. Until you clear your browser data

Third parties we rely on to serve the site may set their own strictly-necessary items:

  • GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) may log technical data such as IP addresses for security.
  • Google Fonts serves the typefaces on this site; your browser requests font files from Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. If we add analytics

If we introduce analytics or marketing cookies in future, they will load only after you accept them via the cookie banner, and this policy will be updated with full details first.

4. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies and local storage in your browser settings at any time. Guides: Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge.
