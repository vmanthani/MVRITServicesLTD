4. How long we keep it

Enquiry data is kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any follow-up, and no longer than 24 months unless you become a client. Client records are retained for the period required by UK tax and company law.

We do not sell personal data. We share it only with:

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct or delete it;

  • data portability, where applicable;
  • withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any right, email [CONTACT-EMAIL]. We respond within one month.
